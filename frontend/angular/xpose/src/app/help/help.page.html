Creating your account

Join our community and start creating today by setting up your account:

  • Step 1: Click the "Sign Up" button on the login screen.
  • Step 2: Provide your email address and create a password.
  • Step 3: Customize your profile with a profile picture.
  • Step 4: Verify your email address to activate your account.
  • Step 5: Explore and enjoy the full range of features available!
 How to Create an Event

Organize your gatherings with ease using Xpose. Here's how to create an event:

  • Step 1: Fill in the event details.

    • Start by entering the event's Title and a Description to give attendees an idea of what it's about.

  • Step 2: Choose the event's Privacy Setting.

    • Select whether you want your event to be Public (visible to everyone) or Private (accessible only to invited guests).

  • Step 3: Add an Event Cover Image.

    • Upload an attractive image that represents your event. It'll catch the eye of potential attendees.

  • Step 4: Specify the Location and Date & Time.

    • Set the event's location using the search bar, and choose the Start and End dates and times.

  • Step 5: Add Tags to your event.

    • Enter relevant keywords or tags to help users discover your event more easily.

  • Step 6: Click the Create Event button.

    • Once all the details are in place, hit the "Create Event" button to publish your event for others to see.

 How to Upload an Image

Add visual appeal to your event by uploading images. Here's how:

  • Step 1: Create or Edit an Event.

    • Start by either creating a new event or editing an existing one.

  • Step 2: Locate the "Event Cover Image" Section.

    • Scroll down to find the section where you can upload an event cover image.

  • Step 3: Click on the Image Upload Button.

    • Look for a button or area that allows you to select or upload an image. It may be labeled "Upload," "Select Image," or similar.

  • Step 4: Choose an Image from Your Device.

    • Click the button and select an image from your device's gallery or file explorer.

  • Step 5: Confirm Your Selection.

    • After selecting the image, confirm your choice and ensure it's suitable for your event.

  • Step 6: Save or Publish Your Event.

    • Once the image is uploaded, save your event changes or publish the event with the new cover image.
